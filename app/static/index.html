for Immich

Add your pet
Click ↓ Import from Immich if Immich already recognizes your pet as a person. Otherwise click + Add pet to start from scratch.
2
Find reference photos
Select your pet and click Find references. Browse the results and add 10–20 clear, varied photos. Avoid blurry shots and frames with multiple animals.
3
Add "not my pets" samples
In the Not my pets panel, click Find candidates. Mark photos that look similar to your pet but are not. Aim for 2–3 times as many negatives as references.
4
Run a test scan
Set the Scan from date 1–2 weeks back and click Scan. Review low confidence results: add correct ones as refs, mark wrong ones as not my pets.
5
Iterate
Repeat steps 2–4 a couple of times. Results typically stabilize after 2–3 rounds.
6
Run the full backfill
Once happy with accuracy, set the scan date to when you got your pet and run the full scan. After that, new photos are tagged automatically every 5 minutes.
